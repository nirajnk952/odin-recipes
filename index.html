Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is rich, cheesy, and full of flavor. It's perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pack lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  3. Mix the marinara sauce with the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the noodles, ricotta, beef sauce, and cheeses.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is easy to make and tastes just like the ones from your favorite pizzeria.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the marinara sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Tacos

Description

These tacos are quick to make and full of flavor, perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in the taco seasoning and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Fill each taco shell with ground beef.
  5. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, and sour cream.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
